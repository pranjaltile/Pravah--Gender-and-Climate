Our Mission

Shape a Sustainable Future

Male

Climate change has different impacts on men and women. Men in certain regions often face challenges in agriculture, fishing, and construction jobs due to changing climates, affecting both livelihoods and community stability.

know more

Female

Women are disproportionately affected by climate change. Empowering women through education and leadership roles in climate policy can drive positive change in mitigating climate-related challenges.

know more

Animals and Birds

Climate change threatens biodiversity by altering habitats and food sources. Many species of animals and birds are at risk of extinction unless steps are taken to reduce global warming and protect ecosystems.

know more

LGBTQ

Climate change affects not only humans and wildlife but also entire ecosystems. Addressing these issues holistically is critical for building a sustainable future for all living organisms on the planet.
