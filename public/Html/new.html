Increased Frequency

Heatwaves are occurring 2-3 times more frequently than in the 1980s. This increased frequency impacts health, the environment, and the economy, necessitating mitigation strategies.

Health Impacts

The health impacts of heatwaves are significant, particularly affecting vulnerable populations. Public health strategies are essential to address these challenges and enhance community resilience.
