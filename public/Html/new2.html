Disruption of Hormone Therapy Access

Understanding how climate change and other factors affect access to hormone therapy for transgender individuals.

Statistics on Disruption of Hormone Therapy Access

Healthcare Access Challenges

Over 35% of transgender individuals have reported difficulty accessing hormone therapy, especially in rural areas.

Supply Chain Issues

Climate events have disrupted the supply chain for hormone medications in over 20% of cases globally. Efficient disaster planning can help ensure access continuity.

Mental Health Impact

The mental health of individuals relying on hormone therapy is deeply impacted by delays or interruptions in treatment. Access to care is vital to mental well-being.
